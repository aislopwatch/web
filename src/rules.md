## What we blame

AISlopWatch documents **AI slop and hype** and blames the **work** — the post,
the listicle, the generated image — not people's private lives. "This is slop"
is our **opinion**, anchored to the screenshot you can see.

- **The output, not the person.** We judge the *thing that was shipped*, not
  anyone's character. We don't accuse people of crimes, fraud, or lies dressed
  up as "opinion."
- **Public figures, brands and companies** are fair game — they put themselves
  forward and must tolerate sharp criticism.

## What we won't do

- **No doxxing, no private data** — no addresses, phone numbers, emails,
  employers, or anything not already public in the post itself.
- **Private individuals get protected.** If the poster is a regular person (not
  a public figure), we **redact** their name, @handle, profile picture, and face.
- **No minors.** Nothing involving anyone who appears to be under 18.
- **No pile-ons.** We blame the work once; we don't organise harassment.
- **Claims are backed by the exhibit.** If you can't see it in the screenshot,
  we don't state it as fact.

## Submitting a sighting

By submitting you confirm: the screenshot is genuine and unaltered, you're not
exposing anyone's private data, and the target is the *content* (or a public
figure) — not a private person's personal life. **Submissions are reviewed
before anything is published.**

<h2 id="removal">Corrections &amp; removals</h2>

Spotted yourself, a factual mistake, a copyright concern, or private info that
slipped through? We take it seriously and act fast.

Email **[{{ site.contact }}](mailto:{{ site.contact }})** with:

- the **link** to the post,
- what you'd like — a **correction**, **redaction**, or **removal**,
- and, if relevant, a line on why (e.g. "that's me, I'm not a public figure",
  or a copyright / DMCA notice).

You **don't** have to prove your identity for us to redact or remove a private
individual. Copyright takedown notices are honoured the same way.

## Data &amp; contact

We publish screenshots of public posts to document AI slop and hype — a matter
of public interest. The site uses **no trackers and no cookies**. For any
data-protection request (objection, deletion) or the operator's contact details,
use the email above.
