# AISlopWatch

**AI is fine. What people do with it often isn't.** A logbook of AI cringe —
screenshotted, labeled, and blamed. We're anti-_lazy_ and anti-_grift_, not
anti-AI; we just collect the receipts.

🔗 **[aislopwatch.com](https://aislopwatch.com)**

## The two crimes

- **Slop** — low-effort, machine-extruded filler that nobody read back before
  shipping. The "Top 10" listicle that's three sentences in a trench coat; the
  blender review written by something that has never owned hands.
- **Hype** — wide-awake humans swearing a chatbot loves them, will replace your
  job by Tuesday, or made them $40k with a single prompt.

Bonus points when a post is both: AI-written cringe about how important AI is.

## How it works

- **Browse** — newest first, or sort by *most-blamed* / *most-viewed*, filtered
  by verdict (slop · hype · guide · tell · meta).
- **Blame it** — hit 🔨 *blame this slop*. One blame per visitor, cookieless, no
  account. The most-blamed bubble up to the
  **[Hall of Shame](https://aislopwatch.com/trending/)**.
- **Comment** — sign in with GitHub and weigh in at the bottom of any post.
- **Submit a sighting** — caught some in the wild? See below.

## Submit a sighting

Screenshot it before they quietly delete it, then
**[open a sighting →](https://github.com/aislopwatch/web/issues/new?template=slop-sighting.yml)**.
Note where you found it, what gave it away, and who profits — exhibits beat
assertions. The community votes 👍 / 👎; winners (or an editor's nod) get
published automatically, credited to you.

> If a human would be embarrassed to have written it, and no human will admit to
> having written it, it is probably slop.

## No trackers, no slop

System fonts only. No webfonts, no third-party scripts, no cookies, no analytics
that watch *you*. The only numbers we keep are **aggregate** blames and views —
we count the slop, not the visitor.
